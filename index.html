Our story

Treat yourself to some time out in LUX KITCHEN, and experience Stockholm in a new way. With its unrivalled setting in the heart of Stockholm, our restaurant will serve you Scandinavian gourmet menu with French and Italian influences and emphasy to fresh local seafood. You’ll be amazed by our culinary delights, its performance marked with an air of elegance.

LUX KITCHEN was created through the initiative of a group of international professionally trained chefs, sommelier and local entrepreneurs. The atmosphere that we create in our restaurant, the products that we offer our clients, are the outcome of that knowledge and the result of our passion and commitment, which is renewed every day.

Artisanal quality since 2002

Our traditions

We believe in flavours and conceive our passion for food as a lifestyle. We believe that beauty and quality can be found in caring for small but important details. The traditions of our country are our main source of inspiration. We value the artisanal way of working, with care, hands and heart. Our food and wine philosophy is in constant evolution. We believe that the customer will enjoy accompanying us in the eye-opening adventure of finding new and exhilarating tastes and stories that can only be experienced at our table.
